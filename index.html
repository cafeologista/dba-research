üîç About This Research

This doctoral study investigates how U.S.-based Property & Casualty insurance companies are integrating Artificial Intelligence into their operations, with a particular focus on:

  • Kaizen and continuous improvement methodologies
  • Leadership strategies for AI adoption
  • Employee engagement and training approaches
  • The intersection of AI technology with operational excellence practices

The research examines multiple P&C sectors including commercial lines, workers' compensation, and other insurance types to understand how AI is transforming workflows, decision-making, and organizational culture.


Research Goals:

  • Understand how leadership drives AI adoption in continuous improvement initiatives
  • Document employee experiences with AI tools and training programs
  • Identify best practices for integrating AI with Kaizen/Lean/Six Sigma methodologies
  • Examine the human dimension of AI transformation in insurance operations

üîê Confidentiality, Ethics, and Compliance

Your Privacy is Protected

  • All survey responses are completely anonymous
  • Company names and identifying information will be coded and de-identified
  • Individual responses will never be shared or attributed
  • Data will be aggregated across multiple organizations
  • No company-specific information will be disclosed without explicit permission

Data Security

  • Survey data stored on secure, encrypted servers
  • Access limited to the research team only
  • All data handling complies with GDPR and academic research ethics standards

Voluntary Participation

  • Participation is entirely voluntary
  • You may skip any questions you're uncomfortable answering
  • You may withdraw from the study at any time without penalty
  • Declining to participate will not affect your relationship with the researcher or institution

Research Compliance

This research complies with academic ethics standards and data protection regulations. Survey responses are collected and stored securely.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How much time does the survey take?

A: Approximately 15-20 minutes. You can complete it in one session or save and return later.


Q: Will my responses be shared with my employer?

A: No. All individual responses are confidential and anonymous. Only aggregated, de-identified findings may be shared.


Q: What types of questions will be asked?

A: Questions cover your experience with AI tools, training programs, workflow changes, leadership approaches, and continuous improvement practices.


Q: Can I participate if my company is just beginning to explore AI?

A: Yes! We're interested in organizations at all stages of AI adoption, from early exploration to scaled implementation.


Q: What if I can't answer all the questions?

A: You may skip any questions you're unable or uncomfortable answering. Partial responses are still valuable.


Q: Will participating take time away from my work?

A: The survey is designed to be completed during a convenient time of your choosing. Many participants complete it during a lunch break or outside work hours.


Q: How will the findings be used?

A: Findings will be published in academic formats (dissertation, journal articles) and shared as summary reports with participating organizations. The goal is to advance industry knowledge about AI transformation.


Q: Can I see the results?

A: Yes! Participating organizations will receive an anonymized summary report of key findings.
