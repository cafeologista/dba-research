Research: AI & Kaizen in P&C Insurance

Doctoral study overview & participation details

Your organization is invited to participate in a research study being conducted for a dissertation at the Swiss School of Business and Management (SSBM).

About the Research

The study examines how U.S.-based Property & Casualty (P&C) insurance are integrating Artificial Intelligence (AI) into their operations, with a particular focus on leadership strategies and the application of Kaizen continuous improvement principles.

The research seeks to capture diverse perspectives form leadership, technology teams, and frontline staff to better understand the enablers, challenges, and outcomes of AI integration.

Participation Includes

  • SURVEY: A short, confidential questionnaire for colleagues in Leadership, Underwriting, and IT involved in transformation initiatives.
  • DOCUMENTATION (Optional): Internal strategy-level documents your firm can share, such as AI adoption plans, process improvement frameworks, or change management materials. These may be anonymized or redacted. All shared materials remain confidential and are used strictly for academic purposes.

You may participate in the survey without providing documentation. If you share documents, please remove sensitive details or provide redacted versions.

Why Your Organization was Selected

Your organization was invited because it meets the following criteria:

  • Operates in the P&C insurance sector
  • Based in the U.S.
  • Has integrated or is in the process of integrating AI into operations

Target Participants and Survey Groups

A total of 15 individuals from your organization will be invited to participate:

  • Five (5) in leadership roles directly involved in AI-related decision-making
  • Five (5) in IT-related roles central to AI implementation processes
  • Five (5) frontline workers/end-users of the implemented AI tool(s)

Timeline

We kindly request that all surveys be completed and submitted within two (2) weeks of signing the consent form.

Survey Tool and Distribution

Surveys will be hosted in Google Forms. Links will be shared directly with the participants or with the authorized representative, who may distribute them internally.

Your Rights and Privacy

  • Participation is voluntary.
  • You may choose not to answer any question.
  • You may withdraw from the study at any time without penalty.
  • There are no known risks associated with participation.
  • All data collected is confidential. No names or identifying information will be linked to responses.
  • Only the researcher will have access to raw survey data.

Benefits

There are no direct personal or financial benefits to participants. However, findings may be valuable to your organization and the broader P&C industry considering integrating AI into their practices by offering insights into successful AI adoption and continuous improvement practices.
